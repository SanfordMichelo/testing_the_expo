About Cosmetology Expo

Zambia's premier exhibition and conference for the beauty, hair, nail, skin and makeup industry bringing together exhibitors, competitors, sponsors and beauty lovers under one roof.

Who We Are

Cosmetology Expo 2026 is a two-day celebration of everything beauty from hair and nails to skin and makeup. We bring together the country's leading professionals, exhibitors and upcoming talent for a showcase of skill, creativity and sustainable beauty practices.

Held on the 14th–15th of July 2026 at ZCAS University the Expo is a space to learn connect and grow whether you're an established brand a student finding your footing in the industry, or simply someone who loves all things beauty.

What to Expect

Exhibitions

Discover brands products and services from across the beauty hair nail and skincare industry.

Competitions

Watch top stylists and beauty professionals compete and showcase their craft live.

Conference

Learn from industry leaders through talks and sessions focused on growing sustainably.
