About The Expo

Cosmetology Expo Zambia 2026 is the country’s leading platform for beauty, skincare, hair innovation, and wellness entrepreneurship. It brings together professionals, students, brands, and innovators shaping the future of the beauty industry in Africa.

Expect live demonstrations, networking opportunities, product launches, masterclasses, and exhibitions from top local and international brands.
