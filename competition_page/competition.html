TEVETA-REGISTERED COSMETOLOGY SCHOOLS

Step Into The Spotlight

The Cosmetology Expo Schools Competition gives student hairdressers, nail technicians, skincare and make-up artists a stage in front of industry judges, exhibitors and the public. Bring your students. Show your training. Take the trophy home.

WHO CAN ENTER

Built For TEVETA Schools

This competition is reserved for technical institutions registered with the Technical Education, Vocational and Entrepreneurship Training Authority (TEVETA) that run accredited cosmetology programmes. If that's your school, we want you on this stage.

  • ✓ Your institution holds a current TEVETA registration.
  • ✓ You offer an accredited course in Hairdressing, Nail Technology, Skincare/Esthetics or Make-up Artistry.
  • ✓ Each school may enter up to 3 students per category.
  • ✓ Entries are accepted until slots fill or the closing date is reached — whichever comes first.
