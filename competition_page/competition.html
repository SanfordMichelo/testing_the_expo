WHO CAN ENTER

Built For TEVETA Training Institutions

This competition is reserved for technical institutions registered with the Technical Education, Vocational and Entrepreneurship Training Authority (TEVETA) that run accredited cosmetology programmes. If that's your training institution, we want you on this stage.

  • ✓ Your institution holds a current TEVETA registration.
  • ✓ You offer an accredited course in Hairdressing, Nail Technology, Skincare/Esthetics or Make-up Artistry.
  • ✓ Entries are accepted until slots fill or the closing date is reached — whichever comes first.
